### MACD: The Trend Detective

The Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) indicator helps traders spot trend changes. Chartscope breaks it down into three simple components:
- **The MACD line** (fast-moving average minus slow-moving average)
- **The signal line** (a smoothed version of the MACD line)
- **The histogram** (bars showing the difference between the two)

Instead of a wall of text, Chartscope uses color-coded arrows and real-time chart overlays to show you exactly when a buy or sell signal appears.
