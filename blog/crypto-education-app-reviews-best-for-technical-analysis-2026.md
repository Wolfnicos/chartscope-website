## Maximizing Your Learning: Tips for Using Crypto Education Apps

### Step-by-Step: Your First Week with a Crypto Education App

**Day 1-2: Learn One Indicator Deeply**
1. Open any crypto chart (BTC/USD works well)
2. Add the RSI indicator
3. Ask your app: "What is RSI showing right now?"
4. Note the current value (0-100)
5. Ask: "Is this overbought or oversold?"
6. Repeat this process 5 times with different coins

**Day 3-4: Add a Second Indicator**
1. Keep RSI visible
2. Add MACD
3. Ask: "Do RSI and MACD agree or disagree?"
4. Look for divergence — when price moves one way but indicators move another
5. This is where most false signals come from

**Day 5-7: Practice Pattern Recognition**
1. Take a screenshot of your chart
2. Use AI Vision to identify candlestick patterns
3. Compare what you see with what the AI identifies
4. Over time, you'll start spotting Doji and Engulfing patterns yourself

### Three Mistakes to Avoid

**Mistake #1: Learning Too Many Indicators at Once**
Stick to RSI and MACD for your first month. Adding Bollinger Bands, Stochastic, and Volume Profile on day one creates confusion, not clarity.

**Mistake #2: Ignoring Timeframe Context**
A pattern on a 15-minute chart means very different things on a daily chart. Always check the timeframe before making a decision.

**Mistake #3: Treating Education Apps as Signal Services**
If an app tells you "buy now," it's not teaching you. Real education explains *why* a setup might work — and why it might fail.
