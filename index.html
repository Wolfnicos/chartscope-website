FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about ChartScope and crypto market learning.

What is ChartScope and how does it work? ▼
ChartScope is an iOS-only crypto education app for beginners. On-device ML classifies market trends (~1ms on your iPhone's Neural Engine). Then AI explains what the indicators mean — through structured insight cards, multi-turn chat, and visual pattern recognition. Every explanation is educational, never a signal.
What's new in version 1.0.3? ▼
Three premium AI features: AI Chat (ask questions about your chart, get multi-turn conversational answers), AI Vision (visual pattern recognition on chart screenshots — identifies Doji, Engulfing, Shooting Star patterns), and Enriched Insights (9 structured cards including volatility, indicator conflicts, risk/reward ratio, and momentum). Faster loading, 9 languages, and improved accessibility.
How is AI Chat different from signal services? ▼
Signal services tell you what to do (buy BTC now). AI Chat explains why things happen (RSI at 42 with bearish EMA cross historically preceded 5-8% drops). You ask questions, you get educational answers with specific numbers and historical context. Never imperatives like "buy" or "sell".
Is AI Vision sending my data somewhere? ▼
AI Vision requires a separate explicit consent (independent from text AI consent). If enabled, only the chart screenshot is sent to a vision LLM for pattern recognition — along with ground-truth indicator values so the AI doesn't need to read numbers from the image. You can disable vision anytime in Settings. Text-based AI and ML work without vision.
Is ChartScope free to use? ▼
ChartScope offers a 3-day free trial with full access to all features. After the trial, you can subscribe monthly at €4.99/month or annually at €47.99/year (20% savings). Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store and can be cancelled at any time.
Is my data secure with ChartScope? ▼
Yes, absolutely. ChartScope is built with privacy first. Your API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain with hardware encryption. All AI analysis runs on-device using Apple Core ML. No personal data, portfolio information, or credentials are ever sent to external servers. We use no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.
Which cryptocurrency exchanges does ChartScope support? ▼
ChartScope currently supports Binance, Coinbase, and Kraken. You can connect your exchange accounts in read-only mode to view your portfolio in one unified interface. We always recommend using read-only API keys for maximum security.
Does ChartScope provide financial advice? ▼
No. ChartScope is strictly an educational tool designed to help you learn about cryptocurrency markets and technical analysis concepts. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or trading signals. Always consult qualified financial advisors before making any investment decisions.
What technical indicators can I learn about? ▼
ChartScope covers the most important technical analysis indicators including RSI (Relative Strength Index), MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence), Bollinger Bands, Moving Averages, Volume Analysis, and common chart patterns like head and shoulders, double tops, and support/resistance levels. Each indicator comes with clear explanations and visual examples.
Can I use ChartScope offline? ▼
The AI analysis features work entirely offline since all machine learning models run on your device. However, you need an internet connection to fetch live market data and sync your exchange portfolio. Cached data remains available offline.
