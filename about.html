About ChartScope

Making cryptocurrency market education accessible, private, and powered by AI.

Our Mission

ChartScope was born from a simple observation: understanding cryptocurrency markets shouldn't require expensive courses or years of experience. We believe everyone should have access to quality educational tools that explain complex market concepts in plain language.

We built ChartScope as an on-device AI companion that teaches you technical analysis while you explore real market data. No cloud processing, no tracking, no data collection. Just pure learning on your iPhone.

🎓

Education First

Every feature is designed to teach. From AI-powered chart explanations to interactive indicator breakdowns, learning is at the core of everything we build.

🔒

Privacy by Design

Your data belongs to you. All AI processing happens on your device. We collect zero personal data, use no analytics, and run no tracking scripts.

⚡

Accessible to All

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to deepen your knowledge, ChartScope adapts to your level with clear, jargon-free explanations.

What We Believe

  • ✓ Transparency matters. We're upfront about what ChartScope is (an educational tool) and what it isn't (financial advice). We'll never blur that line.
  • ✓ Privacy is a right, not a feature. In an era where apps harvest user data, we chose a different path. Everything runs locally on your device.
  • ✓ AI should empower, not replace. Our AI explains concepts so you can make informed decisions yourself. We don't make predictions or recommendations.
  • ✓ Security is non-negotiable. Read-only API keys, iOS Keychain encryption, and zero cloud storage ensure your exchange connections remain safe.
  • ✓ European values. Built in Europe, compliant with EU regulations, and designed with GDPR principles at the foundation.

Important Disclaimer

ChartScope is an educational tool designed to help you learn about cryptocurrency markets and technical analysis. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or trading signals. Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile. Always do your own research and consult qualified financial advisors before making investment decisions. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

Built in Europe

ChartScope is developed and operated from within the European Union. We are committed to complying with EU regulations including GDPR and the European Accessibility Act (EAA 2025). Our privacy-first approach isn't just a feature, it's how we believe all software should be built.

We support the EU's vision for a safer digital ecosystem and actively follow developments in crypto regulation, including the Markets in Crypto-Assets (MiCA) framework, to ensure our educational content reflects the latest regulatory landscape.
