### Candlestick Patterns: The Language of Price Action

Every crypto chart tells a story through candlesticks. Each candle represents price movement over a specific time period — typically 1 hour, 4 hours, or 1 day.

#### The Three Patterns Every Beginner Must Know

| Pattern | What It Looks Like | What It Suggests | Reliability |
|---------|-------------------|------------------|-------------|
| **Doji** | Open and close prices are nearly equal | Market indecision; potential reversal | ~60% when confirmed by volume |
| **Bullish Engulfing** | Green candle fully "engulfs" previous red candle | Strong buying pressure; uptrend possible | ~65% on daily timeframes |
| **Shooting Star** | Small body with long upper wick | Selling pressure after uptrend; potential top | ~55% — needs confirmation |

**Why this matters for Luxembourg beginners:** Candlestick patterns combined with volume analysis improve trade timing accuracy by up to 35% compared to price-only analysis.

### RSI: The Overbought/Oversold Compass

The Relative Strength Index (RSI) measures how quickly prices are changing. It's displayed as a line that oscillates between 0 and 100.

**What the numbers mean:**
- **RSI above 70:** The asset may be overbought (prices rose too fast)
- **RSI below 30:** The asset may be oversold (prices dropped too fast)
- **RSI around 50:** Neutral — no extreme momentum

**Important caveat:** RSI can stay in overbought territory during strong uptrends. It's a warning light, not a stop signal.

### MACD: The Trend Strength Meter

The Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) consists of two lines and a histogram:

- **MACD line:** Shows the relationship between two moving averages
- **Signal line:** A smoothed version of the MACD line
- **Histogram:** The difference between the two lines

**Quick interpretation:**
- When the MACD line crosses **above** the signal line → potential bullish momentum
- When the MACD line crosses **below** the signal line → potential bearish momentum
- Widening histogram bars → strengthening trend
