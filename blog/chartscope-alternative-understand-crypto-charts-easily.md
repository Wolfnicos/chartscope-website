When evaluating a crypto chart education app, focus on features that promote understanding.

1.  **AI-Powered Explanations:** The app should translate complex chart patterns and indicators into plain language. This helps beginners grasp concepts without technical jargon.
2.  **Visual Pattern Recognition:** The app should visually identify common chart patterns like head and shoulders or triangles. This accelerates learning with immediate feedback.
