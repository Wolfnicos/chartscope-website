### What to Consider When Comparing Prices

Not all charting apps are created equal, and price alone shouldn't drive your decision. Here are the key factors to evaluate:

**Feature depth vs. breadth**: A $5 app with shallow education may be worse than a $10 app with deep, interactive learning. Look for apps that explain *why* patterns form, not just *what* they look like.

**Privacy costs**: Free apps often monetize through data collection. If an app is free, ask yourself how they make money. Chartscope's €4.99/month model is transparent — you pay for the app, not with your data.

**Trial periods**: Always use the free trial before committing. Chartscope's 3-day trial is enough to test the AI Chat and AI Vision features thoroughly. If an app doesn't offer a trial, be skeptical.

### Price Comparison Tips for Beginners

- **Start with the cheapest option that has real education**: Don't pay for advanced features you won't use for months. Chartscope at €4.99/month is ideal for beginners.
- **Avoid apps that lock basic education behind premium tiers**: Some apps teach RSI only in their $30/month plan. Look for apps where education is core to the experience.
