Nicolas Wolf

iOS Developer · Crypto Educator · Creator of ChartScope

I built ChartScope because most crypto tools either overwhelm beginners with complexity or push trading signals that teach nothing. My goal is different: explain how charts actually work, so you learn to read the market yourself — not follow someone else's calls. Every article on this site draws from 8+ years of iOS development experience and 5+ years of hands-on crypto market analysis.

Credentials

🛠 iOS Developer (8+ Years)

Building native Apple applications with SwiftUI, Core ML, and on-device machine learning. ChartScope runs entirely on Apple's Neural Engine — zero cloud processing, zero data collection.

📊 Crypto Educator (5+ Years)

Analyzing crypto markets using technical analysis and machine learning. Author of 60+ educational articles on candlestick patterns, indicators, and chart analysis — all beginner-focused.

🔒 Privacy-First Engineer

Privacy is a design principle, not a checkbox. ChartScope processes everything on-device: no analytics SDKs, no user tracking, no data sent to external servers. Your charts stay on your phone.

Operating under EU GDPR and MiCA regulatory frameworks. ChartScope is built for privacy by default — a requirement, not a feature.
