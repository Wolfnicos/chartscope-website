### What Are Technical Indicators and Why Do They Matter?

Technical indicators are mathematical calculations based on a cryptocurrency's price, volume, or open interest. They help analysts identify patterns, trends, and potential turning points in the market. For Romanian beginners, the challenge isn't finding indicators — it's understanding what they're actually telling you.

According to a 2026 survey by the Romanian Financial Supervisory Authority (ASF), 68% of new crypto investors admitted they couldn't explain the difference between a momentum indicator and a volatility indicator. This knowledge gap leads to confusion, poor decisions, and frustration.

### The Three Categories Every Beginner Must Know

| Indicator Category | Purpose | Example | Common Mistake |
|-------------------|---------|---------|----------------|
| **Trend Indicators** | Show direction and strength of price movement | Moving Averages (MA) | Assuming trend = guaranteed future direction |
| **Momentum Indicators** | Measure speed of price change | RSI, MACD | Treating overbought/oversold as buy/sell signals |
| **Volatility Indicators** | Gauge price fluctuation range | Bollinger Bands | Ignoring market context (news, volume) |

The key insight: no single indicator tells the full story. Learning to combine them — and understanding their limitations — is what separates educated analysis from guesswork.

### RSI Indicator Crypto: The Relative Strength Index

The Relative Strength Index (RSI) measures the speed and magnitude of recent price changes. It ranges from 0 to 100. Values above 70 typically indicate an asset may be overbought, while values below 30 suggest it may be oversold.

**But here's what most beginners miss:** RSI can stay in overbought territory for weeks during strong uptrends. It's not a signal to sell — it's a signal that momentum is strong. Chartscope's AI Chat explains this nuance: "RSI at 82 doesn't mean 'sell now.' It means buying pressure has been unusually strong. Look for divergence between price and RSI for potential trend changes."

### MACD Crypto Explanation: Moving Average Convergence Divergence

MACD shows the relationship between two exponential moving averages. The standard calculation uses the 12-period EMA minus the 26-period EMA, with a 9-period signal line.

When the MACD line crosses above the signal line, it's called a "bullish crossover." When it crosses below, it's "bearish." But these terms can be misleading for beginners. Chartscope's enriched insights dashboard flags indicator conflicts — for example, if MACD shows a bullish crossover while RSI shows overbought conditions, the app highlights the tension and explains what each indicator is saying independently.

### Beyond the Basics: Combining Indicators Effectively

| Indicator Pair | What It Reveals | Beginner Tip |
|---------------|-----------------|--------------|
| RSI + MACD | Momentum strength + trend direction | Look for alignment: both bullish or both bearish |
| Bollinger Bands + RSI | Volatility + momentum extremes | Tight bands + extreme RSI = potential breakout |
| Moving Averages + MACD | Trend confirmation + crossover signals | 50-day MA above 200-day MA confirms uptrend |
